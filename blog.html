Reflections on the AI-Energy Nexus

As models grow larger, so does their energy consumption. In this post, I want to explore how the academic community can pivot towards "green AI." It is becoming increasingly clear that unless we incorporate energy metrics natively into our loss functions, we risk offsetting the very environmental gains that AI is supposed to help optimize.
